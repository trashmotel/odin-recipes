Spicy Goat Cheese Avocado Toast

Description

This is a simple, yet delicious avocado toast recipe that doesn't take too long to prepare.

Ingredients

(Serves 1)

  • 1 slice of Sourdough Bread
  • 1/2 of an avocado
  • 3 tbsp fresh goat cheese
  • 1 tbsp Tobasco Habanero or hot sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Toast Sourdough bread in toaster or air fryer at 370Â° for 5 minutes
  2. While bread is toasting, slice avocado lengthwise and set aside
  3. Add goat cheese and hot sauce to a small bowl, combine, and set aside
  4. After the bread is done toasting, remove from the toaster
  5. Spread goat cheese on toast, top with all the sliced avocado, and plate
  6. Enjoy!
