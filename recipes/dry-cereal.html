You LAZY Sack

Too tired to cook a meal? Not enough money for takeout? Look no further, because this recipe is for YOU!!

Ingredients

(Serves 1)

  • 1 Box of your favorite cereal
  • 1 bowl (optional)

Steps

  1. Open Box of Cereal
  2. Open bag inside cereal box
  3. Pour cereal into bowl (optional)
  4. Enjoy.
