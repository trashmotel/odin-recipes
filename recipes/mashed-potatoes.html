Really Good Mashed Potatoes

Description

In the mood for a delicious side dish that will completely destroy your arteries? Following this recipe will ensure that your next doctor's visit will be a stressful one!

Ingredients

(Serves 1)

  • 1/2 lb yukon gold or yellow potatoes
  • 2 oz extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup half&half cream
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • chives for garnish
