Really Good Mashed Potatoes

Description

In the mood for a delicious side dish that will completely destroy your arteries? Following this recipe will ensure that your next doctor's visit will be a stressful one!

Ingredients

(Serves 1)

  • 1/2 lb yukon gold or yellow potatoes
  • 2 oz extra sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup half&half cream OR milk
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • chopped chives for garnish

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into 2 in. cubes
  2. Fill a pot with water and add some salt (about 2 tbsp) and heat atop a stove burner until boiling
  3. Add potatoes to pot of boiling water and let cook until a knife or fork can pierce through them with no resistance (about 15-20 min)
  4. Remove the potatoes from the water via a strainer and let some of the water evaporate
  5. Pass potatoes through a ricer into a mixing bowl OR place all the potatoes in the bowl and use a potato masher until potatoes are desired consistency
  6. Add cream, butter, and garlic powder to bowl and mix using a rubber spatula until homogeneous
  7. While continuing to mix, add grated cheddar cheese until homogeneous
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Transfer mashed potatoes to serving bowl and garnish with chopped chives
  10. Enjoy!
